The History of The StylesHub or TSH

TSH was founded on 25th May 2017

It was an idea of two young best friends Smit Shah and Payal Mehra to start a fashion comapny that not only makes quality material but also gives freedom to the user to design his/her own outfit. The moto of the company was simple 'make everyone reflect their personality' The StylesHub was founded in the city of Kalyan, Maharashtra.
